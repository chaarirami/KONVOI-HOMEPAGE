The Schumacher Group is one of Germany's leading specialist logistics providers for high-value and sensitive goods. With a growing fleet and increasing pressure from organised cargo crime, the company faced a fundamental choice: reactive insurance or preventive security?

KONVOI provided the answer — with an installation in under 120 minutes per vehicle and AI that detects threat patterns before damage occurs.
