Greilmeier Spedition & Logistik operates complex intermodal transports in which trailers change hands multiple times. Attributing damage and providing evidence to insurers and clients previously took days.

With KONVOI and the Logbook add-on, a complete PDF report is generated automatically for every journey — no manual follow-up required.
